\## Project Overview



This project demonstrates how Prompt Engineering can be used to generate high-quality, conversion-focused website copy for local businesses.



The prompt framework was designed to create:



\- Homepage copy

\- Services page content

\- Call-to-action sections

\- Tone-adapted content



The generated content is suitable for real-world business websites.

\## Prompt Engineering Strategy



\### 1. Role Prompting



The AI was instructed to act as a professional website copywriter.



\### 2. Context Injection



Business details such as:



\- Business name

\- Industry

\- Location

\- Audience



were supplied to improve relevance.



\### 3. Structured Output Generation



Prompts were separated into:



\- Homepage generation

\- Service generation

\- CTA generation

\- Tone adaptation



\### 4. Tone Control



Content was generated in:



\- Friendly tone

\- Professional tone

\- Confident tone



\### 5. Conversion Optimization



Prompts focused on:



\- Customer benefits

\- Trust building

\- Clear CTAs

\- Local business relevance
